About Us

Our Vision

Our love and vision centers around God’s people knowing the word of God, living the word of God and ultimately falling in love with God to be His true disciples. Our objective is to teach the word of God in its raw form and allow the HOLY SPIRIT to minister to each of us as HE directs our path. Our discipleship ministry focuses on the life cycles of a disciple from birth to reproducing other disciples.

We have established an environment where believers can get away from traditions and focus on their spiritual growth. We want to foster an environment where you can feel comfortable asking about areas of ministry and not feel pressured to act or look a particular way but are allowed to be who God has created you to be. We are driven by the great commission to make disciples as stated in Matthew 28:19-20. In addition, we are burdened by our Lord and Savior’s description of the righteous as defined in Matthew 25:35-36. Therefore, we are also focused on the needs of others through our outreach into the community and support to ministries that feed the hungry around the world.

Outreach Initiatives

Alamance County Meals on Wheels

ACMW delivers approximately 350 meals a day to elderly over 65, totaling 100,000 meals each year. As there is a waiting list for the program due to a lack of funding, BOLD Ministries provides financial support, delivers meals, and serves on the Board of Directors.

Local Ministries

We try to remain sensitive to other Ministries in our local area and their needs. To the extent we are able, BOLD Ministries provides financial support to enable them to better address their immediate needs.

Haiti

Christian Mission of the New Testament (Mission Chrétienne du Nouveau Testament) established in May 2004 by the Pastors Jean Kenel Many and Wilfrance Pierre. Goals are to Evangelization, Worship, Training, Instruction and Education, Social, Psychological, medical Work and Orphanage

Healing Station

Operates a food pantry that feed over 300 local families per month. Also operates a clothing ministry to families in need and individuals that need career clothing. Their Resource Center is open to assist individuals with job readiness assistance. The also recently partnered with Alamance Community College to provide basic skills classes.

Food for the poor, Inc.

Provides food, housing, emergency relief and much more to those in desperate need fulfilling a special calling and are uniquely equipped to serve the poorest of the poor in the Caribbean and Latin America. BOLD Ministries provides financial support to help enable this organization to provide food to the needed.
