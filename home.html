Every Sunday at 11 AM

We invite you to join us as we gather together to glorify God through fellowship with one another and to study God’s Word, the Bible. We believe the Bible is true and that it tells us how we can obtain new life through a personal relationship with God. Our goal is to become disciples so that we can fulfill the great commission. We are open to worshippers of all ages and backgrounds, and strive to lead all who worship with us towards a deeper love for God.

Children's Ministries
