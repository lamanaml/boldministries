Why Give to this Ministry?

To help support the work of God in our community and around the world

In Scripture, we are often challenged to give to meet the needs of others and to invest earthly treasures in the growth of God’s eternal kingdom. Not only is the act of giving important, but the attitude behind the gift is also important.

“He which soweth sparingly shall reap also sparingly; and he which soweth bountifully shall reap also bountifully. Every man according as he purposeth in his heart, so let him give; not grudgingly, or of necessity: for God loveth a cheerful giver. And God is able to make all grace abound toward you; that ye, always having all sufficiency in all things, may abound to every good work” (II Corinthians 9:6–8)."

Every Christian should faithfully give a titheof his income to his local church. This discipline provides financially for his spiritual leaders and ensures that the work of God is supported in his community. Gifts beyond the tithe may be given to support other ministries, and organizations.

God is generous, and as His children, we should reflect a family resemblance to our Father. As you joyfully steward your resources with wisdom, you can share the bounty of God’s provision with people who are in need. Foodforthepoor

Provides food, housing, emergency relief and much more to those in desperate need fulfilling a special calling and are uniquely equipped to serve the poorest of the poor in the Caribbean and Latin America. Imagine seeing your children slowly starving and having nothing to feed them. In an effect to support this organization BOLD Ministries provides financial support to help enable this organization to provide food to the needed.

Alamance County Meals on Wheels: ACMW

Alamance County Meals on Wheels has been providing home delivered meals since 2010 to seniors that are over 60 years old in our county. ACMW delivers approximately 350 meals a day, totaling 100,000 meals each year. There is a waiting list for the program and that is due to a lack of funding. In an effect to support this organization BOLD Ministries provides financial support, delivers meals, and serve on the board of director in the planning of the ongoing operations.

Haiti:

Christian Mission of the New Testament (Mission Chrétienne du Nouveau Testament) established in May 2004 by the Pastors Jean Kenel Many and Wilfrance Pierre. Goals:

  1. Evangelization
  2. Worship
  3. Training
  4. Instruction and Education

  6. Orphanage

Local Ministries

We try to remain sensitive to other Ministries in our local area and their needs. To the extend we are able, BOLD Ministries provides financial support to enable them to better address their immediate needs.

Healing Station

Operates a food pantry that feed over 300 local families per month. Also operates a clothing ministry to families in need and individuals that need career clothing. Their Resource Center is open to assist individuals with job readiness assistance. The also recently partnered with Alamance Community College to provide basic skills classes.
