We invite you to join us as we gather together to glorify God through fellowship with one another and to study God’s Word, the Bible. We believe the Bible is true and that it tells us how we can obtain new life through a personal relationship with God through Jesus Christ. Our goal is to become disciples so that we can fulfill the great commission. We welcome worshipers of all ages and backgrounds, and strive to lead all who worship with us towards a deeper love for God.

From the Desk of Pastor Barney

Good Morning Faithful Followers of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ!

I hope this note finds everyone well and staying safe. I know this has been a trying time for all of us. However, we should see it as a time that God is allowing us to draw closer to HIM. He said if we would only draw closer to HIM, HE would draw closer to us.

Some of you may have been wondering when we will come together again. This has been on my mind every day since we stop meeting. I am convinced that God is telling me, now is the time. So, we will plan to start meeting again the first Sunday of June.

However, being that most of us are in the age group that we need to be very careful, we will ensure that we are practicing distancing and other safety precautions, such has no handshakes, hugs and appropriate cleaning as well as good ventilation.

I look forward to seeing each of you the first Sunday of June and please stay safe. God bless each of you!

With Love,
