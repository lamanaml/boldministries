Prayer Request

We have established an environment where believers can get away from traditions and focus on their spiritual growth. We want to foster an environment where you can feel comfortable asking about areas of ministry and not feel pressured to ask or look a particular way but are allowed to be who God has created you to be. We are driven by the great commission to make disciples Matthew 28:19-20.

In addition, we are burdened by our Lord and Savior description on of the righteous as defined in Matthew 25:35-36, When I was hungry you gave me meat. Therefore, we are also focused on the needs of others through our outreach into the community and support to ministries that feed the hungry around the world.
